This app is designed for children and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such data, please contact us at mycountinggame@gmail.com.

Third-Party Services

We do not share your information with third parties. The app works offline and does not transmit data.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at mycountinggame@gmail.com.
